## I've moved around

I was born in Dubai 🇦🇪, studied Computer Science and got my first job as a technical recruiter in Bangalore 🇮🇳 back in 2013.

After spending a few months learning Java in the evenings, I finally landed a job at a software agency that specialised in building online retail applications.

Since then I've been building for the web with a variety of tools, across the stack and whilst collaborating with great teams around the world.

## I ❤️ building products and teams

All the way from tiny automation scripts to microfrontends with <b>over 50 Billion</b> renders per day.

I help teams foster an inclusive, high performing culture that enables them to do [innovative](https://en.zalando.de/campaigns/adaptive-fashion/) and [exciting](https://en.zalando.de/campaigns/running-women/) things that [raise the bar](https://en.zalando.de/about-sustainability/) in online fashion retail.
